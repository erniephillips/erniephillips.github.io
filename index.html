Who is Ernie Phillips

About Me

My name is Ernie Phillips. I am a .NET / SharePoint Web Application developer. I currently live in Hopatcong, New Jersey.

To simply put it, I enjoy my work. I love building a website and experiencing that feeling of accomplishment afterward. In most cases I enjoy backend programming but also dabble in front-end as well.

In my spare time, I enjoy spending time with my family and working on small house projects. For anyone thats ever owned a house, you know those projects are never over, so I find plenty to stay busy.
