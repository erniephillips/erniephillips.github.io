About Me

My name is Ernie Phillips, and I reside in New Jersey. I am a proud father of two beautiful little girls and have a wonderful wife who keeps me busy daily. In my pastime, I routinely am studying for my Master's degree in CIS or pursuing my programming studies.

My initial programming passion was constructing mobile Android and Apple applications until I built my first website. My first website changed the focus of my desire for coding. If you would like to view my first Android app (a college independent study project), please visit the links below.
